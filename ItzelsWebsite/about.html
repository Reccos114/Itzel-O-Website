About Me

Hi! I’m Itzel Ortiz, and I began my journey in real estate and property management with Rent Appeal in 2022. What started as a new opportunity quickly became a true passion. I’ve grown to love the dynamic nature of the industry especially the connections I get to make with both customers and investors. I take pride in delivering exceptional service and building strong, lasting relationships. As continue to grow professionally, I’m committed to expanding my knowledge and exploring all areas of real estate. From property management and tenant relations to marketing and listings, I’m always eager to take on new challenges and contribute meaningfully to the teams I work with.

Skills and Expertise

  • Accounting Support: Bill processing, check entries, and managing accounts payable/receivable.
  • HOA Management: Handling violations, disputes, and fee payments.
  • Operations: Inventory tracking, FMLS listings, tenant portal assistance, utility setups, evictions, leasing restrictions, and move outs.
  • Marketing: Property photography, competitive market analysis, and writing compelling property listings.
